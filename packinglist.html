What to Bring

Check out the Mountain Equipment Co-op list for a pretty comprehensive list of things to bring for a camping trip.

Always double check that you have all of your gear!!

Here's a few additional things that I usually bring that are not mentioned in the MEC list:

  • Telescope - the stars are incredible on a clear night, especially if you are deep in the interior!
  • Boxed wine - cans and bottles are not allowed in provincial parks, but plastic bags full of wine are.
  • Packable pillow - this is listed as optional on the MEC site, but I would definitely recommend bringing one. Your neck will thank you in the morning.
