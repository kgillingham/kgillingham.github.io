FAQ

Where do you recommend going?

My three favorite lakes in the park are Opeongo Lake (purple icon), Rock Lake (red icon), and Lake of Two Rivers (green icon). All three are near roads and are therefore easy to access!

What's the best season to go camping?

My favorite time to go camping is during the autumn, for two reasons: no bugs, and the fall colours are beautiful.

Will I be eaten by a bear while sleeping in my tent?

Yes.
